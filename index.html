Behind the lens

I take pictures of places before they become memories.

I'm Omar, a Software Engineer from Erbil with a passion for photography and adventures. Whether it’s nature, street scenes, trips, or architecture, I like capturing the quiet details and the moments that make a place feel alive.

This work is not only about where I am from. It is about noticing where I am, wherever that happens to be.

So I keep carrying a camera through Kurdistan and the world, building a personal archive one frame at a time.

From mountain trails to city streets, and every small discovery in between.
